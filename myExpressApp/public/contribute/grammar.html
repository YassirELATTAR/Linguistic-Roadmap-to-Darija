Contributing to the Grammar of Darija

Description of the Grammar Dataset

The collections/ tables of the grammar contain several aspects of grammar. This includes the following lists:

  1. Adjectives/ Adverbs/ Prepositions/ Pronouns/ Nouns/ Verbs:

    2. Similar to the vocabulary structure, these collections include several examples in Darija and their different spelling forms, arabic darija, and English translation

  2. Definite and Indefinite Articles:

    3. Since darija is basing this aspect on Arabic, the definite article is formed mostly by adding l, dublicating first consonant, or adding e/a before the dublicate consonant.
    So, this collection includes only examples of definite forms and their respective indefinite forms.

  3. Imperatives/ Conjugation in Past and in Present:

    4. For the these three collections, the structure is based on the pronouns. Darija has 8 pronouns: ana, nta, nti, howa, hia, 7na, ntoma, homa. Beside the pronouns, it contains the root of the verb. They cover some of the most used verbs. Of course, for Imperatives, there are only three pronouns: nta, nti, ntoma

  4. Masculine-Feminine-Plural

    5. This collection contains several examples of nouns in singular masculine, plural masculine, singular feminine, plural feminine.

  5. Noun to Verb (Nominalisation)

    6. Last collection in the grammar contains some of the most common cases of verbs and their nominal forms.
